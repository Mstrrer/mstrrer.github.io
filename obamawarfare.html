Controls

The controls are pretty simple yet I have to make a section for them. W, A, S, and D to move your target overlay over a person. It snaps on a grid, so don't worry if its only half on a person, you'll get them anyways. To fire the drone strikes, press the fire button.

Win Conditions

If you eliminate all people with your drone strike weapon, you get a pop-up saying "you are true obama!" Congratulations, you won the game.
