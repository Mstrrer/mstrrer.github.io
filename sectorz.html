Sector Z

Sector Z is a roleplaying game based around the zombie genre. Though it's systems can be used for anything you want. Sector Z is based on many different intertwining systems from other games, media, etc. along with some custom stuff to bring to the table. This will explain the different systems of Sector Z so you have an understanding when you begin playing.
